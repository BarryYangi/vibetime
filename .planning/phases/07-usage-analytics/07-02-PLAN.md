## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-usage-privacy | Information Disclosure | `codex-scanner.ts`, `claude-scanner.ts` | mitigate | Emit allowlisted token/model/timestamp/source fields only; privacy canary tests assert content never leaves scanner. |

</threat_model>
